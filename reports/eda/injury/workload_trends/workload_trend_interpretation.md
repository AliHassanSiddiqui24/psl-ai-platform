# Workload Trend Interpretation Notes

## Objective
Understand bowling workload patterns as an analytical foundation for injury risk modeling.

## Current workload metrics
- balls bowled per match
- overs bowled per match
- total workload
- average workload per match

## Why this matters
Repeatedly high bowling workload may indicate:
- fatigue risk
- reduced recovery time
- elevated injury risk
- performance decline risk

## Current limitations
At this stage, workload is computed only from available ball-by-ball match data.
This does not yet include:
- training load
- fitness data
- medical history
- gym workload
- external tournament workload outside the current dataset

## Current role in project
This is the first foundational step toward:
- rolling workload features
- fatigue index construction
- injury proxy modeling
