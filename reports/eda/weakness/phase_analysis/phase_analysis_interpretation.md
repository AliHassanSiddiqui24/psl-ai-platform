# Phase-wise Batsman Analysis Interpretation Notes

## Objective
Understand how batsman performance changes across innings phases:
- powerplay
- middle overs
- death overs

## Why this matters
A batsman may:
- score quickly in powerplay but slow down badly in middle overs
- survive well in middle overs but struggle in death overs
- have a high dismissal concentration in one specific phase

## Core metrics
- balls faced
- runs scored
- strike rate
- dismissals
- dismissal rate

## Weakness signals
Potential weakness signals may include:
- low strike rate in a phase
- high dismissal rate in a phase
- strong drop in scoring output between phases

## Important caution
Small sample sizes can distort phase-level conclusions.
Future steps should add minimum-ball filters before making strong tactical claims.
